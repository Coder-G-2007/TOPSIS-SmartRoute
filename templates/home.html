About SmartRoute

SmartRoute is a specialized decision support system built to tackle the logistical complexities of hazardous healthcare waste management. By utilizing multi-criteria decision-making frameworks, it ensures compliance, safety, and environmental sustainability.

Key Optimization Features

TOPSIS Algorithm

Ranks alternative transportation routes dynamically by identifying the closest solution to the ideal objective.

Risk Mitigation

Evaluates and minimizes population exposure to infection risks along routes while maintaining structural safety protocols.

Eco-Friendly Routing

Actively minimizes carbon emissions and overall fuel/energy consumption footprints across municipal boundaries.
